About

Sashi is a mathematics educator with a passion for helping students build confidence, curiosity, and analytical thinking — the quiet skills that outlast any single lesson.

A graduate of the University of Dhaka with both a BSc and an MSc in Mathematics, she pairs depth in the subject with a genuine care for how people learn.

She is currently pursuing a Master of Education (MEd) at the Institute of Education and Research (IER), University of Dhaka, deepening her expertise in teaching and learning.

Above all, she is dedicated to creating meaningful educational experiences that encourage lifelong learning and intellectual growth.
